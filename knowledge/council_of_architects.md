# The Council of Architects

The Council of Architects is the supreme governing body of the Nexus Multiverse, responsible for maintaining dimensional stability and enforcing interdimensional law.

## History

Founded in the year 0 AM (After Merge), the Council was established following the Great Convergence - an event that nearly collapsed all realities into a single chaotic dimension.

## Current Members

2. **Kael Nightshade**

   - Age: 847 years

4. **Xanthus the Unbound**
   - Origin: Unknown (possibly Layer Gamma)
   - Specialty: Reality manipulation
   - Age: Unknown

5. **Mira Song**
   - Origin: Universe-333
   - Specialty: Memory weaving

6. **Commander Orion Black**
   - Origin: Universe-101
   - Specialty: Military strategy
   - Age: 892 years
   - Notable achievement: Defended Prime Reality in the Shadow War

7. **The Oracle** (name unknown)
   - Origin: The Void itself
   - Specialty: Prophecy and foresight
   - Age: Timeless
   - Notable achievement: Exists outside normal time

## Headquarters

The Council operates from **The Spire** - a massive structure that exists simultaneously in all realities. It appears as a 1000-story tower made of crystallized time.

## Council Decisions

Major rulings include:
- The Interdimensional Travel Accord (Year 500 AM)
- The Void Quarantine Protocol (Year 1247 AM)
- The Reality Preservation Act (Year 2100 AM)
- The Nexus Point Regulation (Year 2890 AM)
