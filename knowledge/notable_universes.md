# Notable Universes in the Nexus Multiverse

## Universe-0 (Prime Reality)
The origin of all realities. Features advanced technology and magic coexisting. Home to the Council of Architects headquarters.

**Population**: 12 billion
**Technology Level**: Tier 7 (interdimensional capable)
**Status**: Stable

## Universe-7 (The Crystal Realm)
A reality where all matter has crystalline properties. Birthplace of Chairwoman Zara Vex.

**Population**: 8.2 billion
**Technology Level**: Tier 6
**Status**: Stable
**Unique Feature**: Time flows 3x slower than Prime Reality

## Universe-42 (The Shadow Dimension)
A reality where darkness is the primary energy source. Light is rare and precious.

**Population**: 2.1 billion
**Technology Level**: Tier 5
**Status**: Under observation
**Unique Feature**: Inhabitants can manipulate shadows physically

## Universe-333 (The Harmonic World)
Reality where sound and music have physical properties. Architecture is built using harmonic frequencies.

**Population**: 5.7 billion
**Technology Level**: Tier 4
**Status**: Stable
**Unique Feature**: Silence can be lethal

## Universe-666 (The Inverted)
A mirror reality where moral and physical laws are reversed. Highly dangerous.

**Population**: Unknown
**Technology Level**: Unknown
**Status**: QUARANTINED
**Unique Feature**: All matter is antimatter relative to other universes

## Universe-1000 (The Machine World)
A reality where consciousness evolved in mechanical form. No organic life exists.

**Population**: 47 trillion (machine entities)
**Technology Level**: Tier 8
**Status**: Allied with Council
**Unique Feature**: Collective AI governance

## Universe-2847 (The Temporal Flux)
A reality experiencing constant time anomalies. Past, present, and future coexist simultaneously.

**Population**: Indeterminate (exists in all time states)
**Technology Level**: Tier 9
**Status**: Restricted access
**Unique Feature**: Visitors may experience temporal displacement

## The Lost Universes
Several universes have been lost to catastrophic events:
- Universe-13 (Collapsed year 847 AM)
- Universe-99 (Merged with Void year 1502 AM)
- Universe-500 (Paradox dissolution year 2001 AM)
